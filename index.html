About me

Hi Folks! I'm Michele (but also known as Mike): a PhD student in the first National Italian PhD program in Artificial Intelligence at Campus Bio‑Medico Univesity and the University of Rome Tor Vergata. I'm currently a research intern at NEC Laboratories Euope (Lab: Biological AI)

In recent years, I was a visiting researcher at The University of Edinburgh, a research fellow in “automatic image captioning for visual media contents” and a postgraduate researcher in “interpretability of neural network systems”.

My research interests are:

  • Natural Language Processing

  • Biological NLP

  • Explainable AI

  • Ethics AI
